clouddrums

The virtual session drummer.
clouddrums.com is the world's first virtual session drummer - a versatile random beat generator with endless possibilities.

Jam and Record.

clouddrums.com's unique beats are tailored to your needs. The variety of kits allows musicians to jam to beats of virtually any genre. Jam to original beats played by your own personal studio drummer.

Freestyle and Train.

Rappers can practice and freestyle to personalized beats that are one-of-a-kind. clouddrums.com has been used in recording and even as a training exercise for drummers in which they have to listen to a random beat and play it back.
